Social Learning

Learning is a part of life. We learn a lot of different facts, figures, and formulas, but in the US there is not an extensive curriculum that revolves around real financial education. Considering, that now about 25% of the trading volume is from individual investors that is rather alarming. However, sometimes those topics are off limits in social settings or amongst close relations. Fintellects wants to change that and make financial knowledge a social experience.
